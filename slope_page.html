Personal Pick: Vemdalen, Sweden

Vemdalen is pretty much the only slope iv'e ridden for the past 17 years, with some minor exceptions. I have the most experience with this slope, which is why it is my personal pick.

It isn't the biggest of resorts, but it's honest work. The terrain parks are unfortunately not that good and mostly ridden by little kids, but the rest of the mountain is a blast.

Mammoth mountain, USA

As a fellow park rider myself, i am of course going to put Mammoth mountain on the list, as it holds the biggest terrain park in the world!

The mountain itself it huge, with a lot of variety in it's terrain. What sets it apart is their terrain parks. With over a dozen with unique and different obstacles, it's a dream to ride. It also has other fun things to do such as gondola rides and "woolly's tube park".

Väsjöbacken, Sweden

I cannot stress enough how absolutley fantastic Väsjöbacken is, and iv'e never even ridden it!
It's a huge resort, with an astonishing 3 slopes to choose from, and none of them are steep! Their park is insane, with obstacles not from this earth. Iv'e also heard they've manufactured a gravitational force field around Väsjöbacken that prevents people inside from getting hurt, so you can full send it everytime! It also has a heavenly view of Sollentuna!
